WHAT DO WE DO?

Two friends in search of new challenges, in 2022 come together in the city of Bray, to offer carpentry services with the vision of creating efficient, low-cost and high value-added solutions for their clients.

  • Our approach: We bring our knowledge to our clients. We focus on solving problems to improve the efficiency of the spaces and comfort of your home and business, reducing material and labor costs.

  • Our mision: Always available to help you maintain the look of your home or your artwork.

  • Our vision: We are committed to provide the highest quality service for the best value. Our number one priority has always been customer satisfaction and we do so from start to finish.

With over 20 years of experience, Georgy and Rowen offer a wide range of carpentry, remodeling, painting and construction services for home users and small businesses to the Greater Dublin area and its surrounding cities and communities.

However, we also take on projects across the state, so feel free to contact us even if you are not in Dublin.
